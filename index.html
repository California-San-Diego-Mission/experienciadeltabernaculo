Have you ever wondered what the tabernacle of ancient Israel was like? Come experience this once in a lifetime opportunity to take a free tour of a life size, accurate replica of the ancient tabernacle as described in the book of Exodus. You will learn about the customs of the ancient Israelites and how the Old Testament ordinances were practiced.

Explore the welcome center, take a guided tour through the Tabernacle, and introduce your children to the Tabernacle through interactive activities in the Children’s Corner. The full Experience is approximately 45 minutes in length and is appropriate for all ages.
